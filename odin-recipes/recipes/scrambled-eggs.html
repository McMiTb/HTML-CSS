Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs is one of many ways to prepare eggs. With the right technique it is possible to create a very fluffy and creamy substance of just egg, which not only tastes incredible but is also as soft to use in different dishes or just to put on your bread.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • salt and pepper
  • a bit of butter

Steps

  1. put the butter in the pan
  2. heat the stofe to middle heat
  3. after the butter melted add the eggs
  4. mix it until you can see one clear substance
  5. let it fry while constantly stirring
  6. when the eggs form a half firm substance remove the heat and serve the eggs
