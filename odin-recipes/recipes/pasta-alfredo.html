Pasta Alfredo

Description

As one of the easiest to make pastas, Pasta Alfredo an absolut icon among noodle lovers. With only 2 ingredients needed and a cooking time of no longer than 10min it is the perfect for a fast but splendid meal. But not only its cooking time and ingredient-list can convince it fans. Through the use of butter and cheese you get a very creamy sauce, keeping your pasta engulfed perfectly in order to not have any places without it. All the while the cheese is melting on your tongue.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (Fettucine, Spagetthi or Linguine)
  • butter
  • grana padano or pecorino
  • bit of salt and pepper

Steps

  1. boil a pot of water, add some salt and finally your pasta
  2. grate your cheese
  3. put the butter in the pan on middle heat and let it melt
  4. afterwards put a scoop of pastawater in the pan and mix it till it's a creamy mixture (here you can also add some pepper)
  5. when the pasta is ready put it with noodle tongs in the pan and mix it thoroughly
  6. put another scoop of pasta water in the pan
  7. turn of the heat
  8. add the cheese and mix it fast
  9. e voula, here you go
